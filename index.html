About Us

There is a vast disconnect between sustainable energy production and the available storage methods.

  • The energy storage market is fragmented and rather unregulated, specially at the residential level.
  • No single dominant player is taking care of all energy storage needs.
  • Current solutions usually employ a hybrid model built with parts from various sources.

The need of the hour is to bridge this disconnect and bring to the people a reliable and all-encompassing sustainable energy generation and storage solution, which is exactly what we aspire to deliver at Ezinore.

Product

Our product ‘SPARKO’ aims to streamline sustainable energy production as well as storage. Sparko comes in two form factors, namely, Sparko-H for energy storage and Sparko-HS for both the generation and storage of energy.

SPARKO

The Sparko uses a Li-ion battery to store energy generated from solar panels as well as electrical grids to power your home and office appliances. In the ‘HS’ variant, an integrated system of solar panels is built into the ‘H’ model of the Sparko to also fulfill energy generation needs. Sparko uses bifacial solar panels to generate more power per unit area to ensure maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

The Sparko app is an all-in-one package providing the user crucial real-time information like the battery capacity, battery health, temperature, solar panel output, a manual for self-cleaning of panels, etc.

SPARKO is the ultimate sustainable and accessible energy production and storage solution.
